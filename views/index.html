Company Info:

Pets-R-Us is a self-started company that was created 10 years ago to groom dogs. Since then, the company has grown to serve all pets in multiple areas such as grooming, boarding, training, and more. We specialize in helping all animal personalities and make any accommodations necessary so that we do not have to turn away any pets.
